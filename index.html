What We Do

Comprehensive Security Services

CCTV Security Systems

FJ Security Solutions provides commercial-grade CCTV systems for homes and businesses using top brands like Hikvision and Dahua. Our wired systems store footage on an NVR, allowing you to view live and recorded video through a mobile app.

Alarm Systems

FJ Security Solutions supplies, installs, and maintains high-quality alarm systems for both homes and businesses. We offer wired, wireless, and hybrid systems that can be customized to suit your specific needs.

Intercoms

The systems allow you to see who is at your door or gate and, through the mobile app, speak to visitors even when you are not home. You can customize the system to suit your needs, giving you full control.

WiFi & Data Cabling

We provide structured cabling and network setups to ensure fast, stable, and reliable connectivity throughout your property. Each system is tailored to suit your needs, delivering strong performance.
